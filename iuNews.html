Silicon Chip Implant Marvel

Developed by professionals of Indiana University, a new breakthrough medical technology is projected to revolutionize medical treatment practices. The Silicon Chip Implant uses electronic pulses to modify the molecular makeup of cells located in treatment areas. Many injuries once seen irreversible could be treated with this new advancement.

Multiple factors set this method apart from what is common in the medical industry today. Methods of the past required elaborate lab procedures, where the Chip can treatment is essentially instantanious. Developers are confident that this new invention will significantly impact the greater medical industry.

Legal Implications, IU School Of Medicine

Following the project's over five year development period, the IU team is currently seeking approval from the FDA expected within the next year. This will allow for clinical testing and research on people involving a variety of applications including muscle, organ, and nerve damage.

Other Areas of Research

A huge breakthrough is underway in detecting cancer. The FDA has approved a AI-powered device that can detect three of the most common types of skin cancer. Skin cancer is very common in the United States, and this is the first reliable method developed for accuratete detection.
